Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of educators and technologists, EduTech was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make high-quality education accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

Our founders, having experienced firsthand the barriers to quality education in underserved communities, set out to create a platform that would level the playing field and provide world-class learning opportunities to all.

Today, we're proud to serve over 2 million learners across 150+ countries, offering everything from professional development courses to academic subjects, all designed with cutting-edge pedagogy and technology.
